Screen

How will the world appear?

According to sources close to Google, the glasses will have a small LCD or AMOLED screen on the top and in front of the user's eyes. With the use of a camera and GPS, you can get situate, as well as select options with the movement of the head.

Camera

Film and take photos at any time!

With Google Glass it will be possible to take photos with up to 5 megapixels and record videos with 720 lines of resolution. The first videos and photos captured with the device has already started to circulate through the network, but so far nobody has many technical information.

Sensors

The sensitivity of simple glasses:

Who thinks that to run Google Glass will need a keyboard and mouse, ifdeceives sadly. The device comes with several types of sensors and microphones embedded. So, to give a command, just speak or swipe the side of the glasses.

Bateria e Gadgets:

What devices complement Glass?

According to Google itself, Glass will come with a battery that has autonomy enough to last a whole day. Just some activities like videoconferences and long films will require a little more. In addition it comes with WiFi, Bluetooth, 3G / 4G and more.
